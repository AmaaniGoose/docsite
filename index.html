DR. FAKHRA MASROOR, M.B.B.S, DNB (Obs & Gyn), FIAOG Consultant Gynaecologist and Laparoscopy Surgeon

BRIEF PROFILE:
Dr Fakhra Masroor, Consultant Gynaecologist and Laparoscopy Surgeon is an experienced Gynaecologist and Laparoscopic Surgeon with 15 years of experience in the field of Obstetrics & Gynaecologist, infertility and laparoscopic surgery. She has 8 years experience for working as Sr. Residents /Medical Officer (Sr. Surgeon) in Medical Colleges of West Bengal (NRS Medical College and CNMC, Kolkata). She has completed her 3 years DNB training from B.R. Singh Hospital, Eastern Railway, Kolkata.
She has gained immense experience to handle complicated High-Risk Obstetrics and Gynaecological cases. With her vast experience in the field of Obstetrics and Gynaecology, she can handle all type of complicated cases, high risk pregnancy as well as gynaecological cases, and laparoscopic surgeries with total satisfaction to patients. She has attended many workshops and conference in Medical Education and keeps herself updated in latest knowledge and surgical skill in the field of Gynaecology and Laparoscopy surgeries.
She got laparoscopic training from Dr. Das Mahapatra Spectrum as well as from Dr. Dipak Limbachya from Ahmadabad. She also got Hands on training from world fame Dr Osama Shawski for operative hysteroscopy.
She has got training in USG especially TVS (Trans-vaginal sonography) under Prof B N Chakraborty of IRM, Kolkata.
She takes keen interest in preventive measures for the occurrence of disease such as weight control, personal hygiene, screening for cancer, vaccination for cancer prevention.
She understands the medical need of women and treats her patients with holistic view to provide her patients best medical treatment at affordable Cost.
